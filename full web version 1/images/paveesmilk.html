Help

FAQ

  • What is Raw milk?
    Raw milk—straight from the cow—does not go through the pasteurization process.
  • Difference between Raw and Pasteurized milk?
    Raw milk is unprocessed while pasteurized milk has been heated to eliminate harmful bacteria.
  • Can we consume Raw milk?
    Raw milk can be consumed but it's important to ensure it's from a safe and clean source.
  • Benefits of drinking Raw milk?
    Raw milk contains more natural enzymes and nutrients compared to pasteurized milk.
  • What is the pricing?
    Pricing of raw milk may vary based on location and source.
